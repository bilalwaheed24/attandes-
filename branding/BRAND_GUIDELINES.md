# SAMS - Brand Guidelines

## Organization Identity

**Smart Attendance Management System**
An enterprise-grade attendance tracking solution for modern organizations.

---

## Brand Colors

| Color | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|-------|-----|-----|-------|
| Primary Blue | #3b82f6 | 59, 130, 246 | Main brand color, buttons, links |
| Success Green | #10b981 | 16, 185, 129 | Success states, confirmations |
| Error Red | #ef4444 | 239, 68, 68 | Errors, warnings, deletions |
| Background | #f9fafb | 249, 250, 251 | Application background |
| Text Dark | #111827 | 17, 24, 39 | Primary text |
| Text Light | #6b7280 | 107, 114, 128 | Secondary text |

---

## Typography

**Font Family**: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', 'Roboto'

### Font Sizes
- **H1 (Heading 1)**: 32px, bold
- **H2 (Heading 2)**: 24px, bold
- **H3 (Heading 3)**: 20px, bold
- **Body**: 16px, regular
- **Small**: 14px, regular
- **Caption**: 12px, regular

---

## Logo Usage

- **Logo File**: `/branding/logo.txt`
- **Minimum Size**: 40x40px
- **Clear Space**: 10px minimum around logo
- **Backgrounds**: Works on light and dark backgrounds

---

## Voice & Tone

- **Professional**: Business-appropriate language
- **Clear**: Simple, easy to understand
- **Helpful**: Supportive and user-friendly
- **Trustworthy**: Secure and reliable

---

## UI Components

### Buttons
- Primary: Blue (#3b82f6)
- Success: Green (#10b981)
- Danger: Red (#ef4444)
- Disabled: Gray with reduced opacity

### Forms
- Input Height: 44px
- Padding: 12px
- Border: 1px solid #e5e7eb
- Focus: Blue border + shadow

### Cards
- Background: White
- Shadow: 0 4px 6px rgba(0,0,0,0.1)
- Padding: 24px
- Border-radius: 8px

---

## Photography & Imagery

- Professional and clean aesthetic
- Use real data and examples
- Consistent with brand colors
- High quality (1x and 2x resolution)
